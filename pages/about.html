Our Journey • Since 2012

Building a Better World for Them

RescueHope isn't just a shelter; it's a movement dedicated to rewriting the stories of thousands of animals through compassion and community action.

Our Story

Started to save one life at a time.

Founded in 2012, RescueHope began as a small group of friends in a garage, worried about the rising number of stray animals in our neighborhood. What started as local advocacy quickly grew into a full-scale rescue operation.

Today, we operate a network of over 100 foster homes and have rescued thousands of animals. Our goal remains the same: compassionate care and finding the perfect 'forever' for every animal.
