Our Opportunities

Volunteer Opportunities

There are many ways to lend a hand, regardless of your skill level or background.

Animal Care

Help with walking dogs, socializing cats, cleaning kennels, and basic grooming. Direct interaction with our furry residents.

Event Support

Assist at adoption fairs, fundraisers, and community outreach programs. Help us spread the word about our mission.

Administrative

Lend your professional skills in social media, data entry, photography, or grant writing from the comfort of home.

Our Simple Step Process

1

Online Application

Tell us about your interests and availability through our simple registration portal. It only takes 5 minutes.

2

Orientation

Attend a brief session (available online or in-person) to learn our safety protocols and organizational mission.

3

Hands-On Training

Shadow an experienced volunteer for a few shifts to get comfortable with your chosen role and resident animals.
