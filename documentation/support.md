# Support Information

If you encounter any issues or have questions regarding the RescueHope template, we are here to help.

## Getting Help
- **Bug Reports**: Please open an issue in the project's repository. Outline the steps required to reproduce the bug.
- **Assistance**: For general usage questions or integration help, review the existing documentation files.
- **Customization Requests**: Standard support does not cover bespoke customizations or backend integration. Support is intended for fixing template-specific layout, styling, or structural bugs.

## Contact Us
You can reach out to our template support team via email at `support@rescuehope.example.com`. Please include your template version (currently v1.0.0) in the subject line.
